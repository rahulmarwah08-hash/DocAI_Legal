Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Doc AI ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Doc AI mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Conversation Data: Messages you send to our AI assistants are processed to generate responses. We do not permanently store conversation content on our servers.

Uploaded Files: Images and documents you upload for AI analysis are temporarily processed and automatically deleted within 5 days. We do not retain copies.

Device Information: We may collect anonymous device identifiers, operating system version, and app version for crash reporting and service improvement.

Purchase Information: Subscription purchases are processed through Google Play or the Apple App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our AI health companion service
  • To process your subscription purchases
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app stability
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

We use OpenAI's API to process your messages and generate AI responses. Your messages are sent to OpenAI's servers for processing. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy for their data handling practices.

Subscription payments are processed by Google Play and/or Apple App Store under their respective privacy policies.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Conversation data is stored locally on your device. Uploaded files are automatically deleted from our processing servers within 5 days. You may delete your local conversation history at any time from within the app.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for all data transmitted between the app and our servers. Locally stored data is protected by your device's native security features.

6. Children's Privacy

Doc AI is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any data associated with your use of Doc AI by contacting us. You may delete your local conversation history directly within the app at any time.

8. Medical Disclaimer

Doc AI provides general health and wellness information only. It does not provide medical diagnoses, treatment plans, or clinical advice. AI-generated responses should not be considered a substitute for professional medical consultation. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical concerns. In case of emergency, contact your local emergency services.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of material changes through the app or by updating the date at the top of this page.
